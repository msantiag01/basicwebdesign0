About Oaxaca: Food and Events

Oaxaca, Mexico is known for its vibrant culinary scene, featuring an array of delicious dishes that draw inspiration from the region's rich history and diverse cultural influences. Here are a list of dishes that you must try if you are planning to visit.

  • Tlayudas
  • Mole
  • Mezcal
  • Roasted Chapulines

In addition to its delicious cuisine, Oaxaca has many events and festivals throughout the year to celebrate the region's vibrant culture. Here are a list of popular events that you might want to add to your calendar.

  • The Guelaguetza Festival (End of July-early August)
  • Virgin of Guadalupe Festival (December 11)
  • Oaxaca Carnival (February)
  • Night of the Radishes (December)
