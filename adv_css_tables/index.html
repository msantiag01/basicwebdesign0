Welcome to See Oaxaca

This page will inspire you to discover the unique and captivationg state of Oaxaca! Situated in southern Mexico, Oaxaca is a region of incredible diversity, with a rich cultural heritage, stunning natural landscapes, and a culinary tradition that is celebrated around the world. Look in the Gallery tab to see a collection of captivating photos that will transport you to the heart of Oaxaca, showcasing its vibrant street life, breathtaking landscapes, and ancient architecture. About Me

Hello good humans! My name is Michelle Santiago and I am 20 years old. I am currently a part-time college student in Tennessee. Traveling is something I have always wanted to do growing up.

Oaxaca, Mexico was actually the first place I went to when I got my passport. Traveling outside of the country for the first time was scary. Even though I had gone to different state and even adventured out west, I had never been far south before. However, once I was there I started getting the hang of things pretty fast and fell in love with traveling. It really opened my eyes on how big the world is and how even thousands of miles away from home, I still found comfort here. My goals for the future are to travel to other states of Mexico and countries and share my stories from traveling.
